GAMING LAN CENTER

This is a logo I made while working at Lvl Up Expo 2019. This is a new business that will be opening in Las Vegas. A gaming center that also serves dim sum. Thus the name SUMESPORTS

This project is still ongoing and currently paused due to covid-19

  • Client: anthony garcia
  • Company: sumesports
  • Time: less than 24hrs - I was really feeling myself that day.

Why this logo?

Why not in small projects?

I was really feeling real confident this weekend. Lvl up was demanding and I was poping out content no problem. Anthony, the client, wanted to hire me earlier and visited me at my station. He was only joking when he asked if I could work on it during the event. I was feeling incredibly creative and accepted the challenge. We completed everything the same day. Whole logo, branding guide, mocks, everything.


This date is very important to be because I had been dealing with imposter syndrome long after earning my degree. Asura had just hired me the week before and my first day would be the following Monday. The amount of work I did and at the pace I was going during Lvl Up made me realize my value as a designer. Any new work I earn now, I'm not worried, I am excited.
