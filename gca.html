Galapagos Conservation Action

To provide support, funding and action for the adaptive management needed to conserve and restore the unique biodiversity of Galapagos.

The conservation of the critically endangered Mangrove Finch and biological control of the highly invasive blackberry plant known as Mora are the current projects GCA are working on. Learn about their projects to help conserve and restore the unique biodiversity of Galapagos.

I love working for charities. Charities always have amazing photographers. So listen when I say I DID NOT TAKE THE PHOTOS SEEN ON THIS SITE! As much as it makes me happy that people think my photography skills are on par with the photographers credited on GCA, I did not take them. Designing this site was very easy thanks to those amazing photos. These images were cropped and slighty photoshopped, by me, to fit the design. GCA was made on wordpress' free software. Designed and built on a local machine. Then pushed to the client's online hosting. I handled all the front-end and back-end.
