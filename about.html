HELLO I'M JUAN

When I really think about it graphic design has nearly been apart of my entire life. Ever since my first computer running windows 98, I would spend countless hours on Paint digitizing my creations. Being the youngest of seven children my brothers' art were literally years ahead of mine. Jealous of their skills, I would take their sketches and I recreate them with my own style. At the time I didn't believe it when one of my brothers said that people get paid to do this.

Accidents

My life is full of accidents that would later define my future for the better. How I got my first job, being in a car that flew off the freeway, missing high school registion, my birth, etc.. When I missed registering for classes my junior year of high school I was forced into that last elective available, digital arts, and I loved it. I would take the advanced course my senior year and knew very well what I wanted to major in after graduation.

Sometimes I wonder if I would be where I am now without all these "accidents" ever happening. Well for starters, I definitely wouldn't be alive, but that's not the point I am trying to make. The point is that behind all this good luck I have worked incredibly hard for every opportunity I was given.
